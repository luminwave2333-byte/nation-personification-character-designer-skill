# Five-Nation Seed Library

用途：为中、美、英、法、俄角色提供“研究方向 + 设计变量”。不是固定人设。

---

# 1. China / 中国

## Design Vector

```yaml
core: civilization_writing_x_state_ritual_x_controlled_force
shape_language: [vertical, S_curve, diagonal_break]
primary_material_candidates: [silk, ink-like textile, metallic thread, lacquer-like accents]
modern_bridge_candidates: [tailoring, diplomatic_formality, industrial_metal]
prop_line: diagonal
surface_density: medium_high
```

## Candidate cultural systems

- Chinese calligraphy as visual art and graphic rhythm
- historical court textiles and motif hierarchy
- dragon imagery in contexts of authority and cultural symbolism
- silk, embroidery, kesi, metallic-thread effects

## Avoid

- all-dynasty collage
- generic “ancient hanfu” with no era
- all-red qipao as automatic national answer
- dragon on every surface
- tiny pseudo-Chinese text

## Original concept directions

### A. 墨律外交官

现代长礼服式西装，裙/袍片用书写竖势切割；龙不画全身，只用 S 形金属缝线和剑格表达。

### B. 青铜文书官

现代无袖长外套 + 青铜器轮廓扣件 + 竹简式分段腰甲；核心不是龙，而是“文字与制度”。

### C. 山河测绘者

现代测绘/外交风长外套，衣摆结构来自山水画留白与卷轴展开逻辑；道具为卷轴式机械测绘尺。

# 2. United States / 美国

## Design Vector

```yaml
core: industrial_mobility_x_aviation_x_mass_modernity
shape_language: [T_shape, forward_diagonal, star_point_microgeometry]
primary_material_candidates: [leather, denim, nylon, brushed_aluminum]
modern_bridge_candidates: [aviation, spaceflight, city_neon, broadcasting]
prop_line: forward_diagonal
surface_density: medium
```

## Evidence-friendly directions

- aviation clothing and flight jackets
- individual customization through patches/artwork
- denim and workwear as material language
- aerospace hardware and brushed-metal surfaces

## Avoid

- full-body Stars and Stripes
- cowboy + eagle + Statue of Liberty + NASA + hamburger in one character
- bald eagle head or racial caricature

## Original concept directions

### A. 高空指挥员

短飞行夹克、硬肩、导航护臂、翼型护手；红白蓝通过内衬/主体/缝线分工。

### B. 广播时代主持人

1940s–50s 演说与广播几何桥接现代舞台服；麦克风架变成武器轮廓。

### C. 轨道维修员

工作服/牛仔与航天结构混合；星形只进入铆钉分布和关节扣件。

# 3. United Kingdom / 英国

## Design Vector

```yaml
core: ceremony_x_tailoring_x_rebellion
shape_language: [I_shape, H_shape, vertical, cross_diagonal_seams]
primary_material_candidates: [wool, tweed, leather, polished_silver_metal]
modern_bridge_candidates: [punk_deconstruction, city_tailoring, trench_structure]
prop_line: vertical
surface_density: low_medium
```

## Evidence-friendly directions

- traditional tailoring
- wool/tweed material culture
- military and trench-derived garment structures
- historical reworking and subcultural deconstruction

## Avoid

- top hat + monocle + tea cup as entire design
- Union Jack pasted on chest
- tartan everywhere without regional context

## Original concept directions

### A. 礼仪破坏者

极端精确的长裁剪外套，内衬交叉斜线呼应旗帜几何；单侧下摆被“拆缝”，金属链成为节奏。

### B. 雾都测时官

I 型长外套，钟表/航海测量不是贴图，而是扣件和关节几何；伞杖形成唯一垂直道具。

### C. 海风裁缝

海军式双排扣逻辑与高定长外套结合，厚毛呢，内层少量格纹，长尾在风中形成动作。

# 4. France / 法国

## Design Vector

```yaml
core: couture_x_republican_color_x_revolutionary_cut
shape_language: [X_shape, sculptural_A, arc, asymmetric_volume]
primary_material_candidates: [satin, organza, fine_wool, enamel]
modern_bridge_candidates: [haute_couture, art_deco_geometry, urban_fashion]
prop_line: arc
surface_density: high_controlled
```

## Evidence-friendly directions

- Paris couture and sculptural garment construction
- blue/white/red national color references translated into fashion construction
- historical silhouettes filtered through modern couture

## Avoid

- beret + baguette + Eiffel Tower
- rococo costume copied directly
- equal-width tricolor stripes

## Original concept directions

### A. 三色切割师

白色主体，蓝色承担结构阴影，红色只出现在切口和移动内衬；裁剪尺与细剑融合。

### B. 革命舞台监督

18 世纪体量感只保留腰臀比例，其他全部现代化；胸前使用结饰几何而非直接徽章。

### C. 珐琅弧线官

雕塑长裙，弧形肩片与珐琅扣件；道具是折扇/细剑混合的弧线装置。

# 5. Russian Federation / 俄罗斯

## Design Vector

```yaml
core: winter_mass_x_festival_craft_x_monumental_order
shape_language: [column, heavy_A, vertical_mass, fan_headpiece]
primary_material_candidates: [wool, velvet, fur_edge, metallic_thread, enamel]
modern_bridge_candidates: [constructive_geometry, industrial_hardware, formal_longcoat]
prop_line: heavy_vertical
surface_density: medium_high
```

## Evidence-friendly directions

- sarafan long straight flowing lines
- kokoshnik structural head silhouette
- regional variation in ornament and materials
- wool, velvet, gold embroidery, pearls/gem-like decoration in historic festive garments

## Avoid

- “Russian = fur hat + vodka + bear”
- copying ceremonial or wedding dress without context
- treating one regional folk costume as the whole nation

## Original concept directions

### A. 冬宫碑彲

柱状长外套为高额部头件，厚毛呢+天鹅绒，装饰只集中在头部和胸前；下半身大面积留白/暗面。

### B. 红线构成者

sarafan 的长直线被转成现代长面板，红色构成线切割深色主体，头件采用扇形结构但极简。

### C. 北地礼仪官

厚 A 轮廓，毛皮只做边缘保温逻辑，金线只在肩胸一处；长柄道具强化纪念碑感。
